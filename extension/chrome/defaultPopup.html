How to Use Eat Word

  • 1
    Select Text: Highlight any text on a webpage (up to 1,000 characters) to translate it instantly.
  • 2
    Choose Your Method: Click the floating Eat Word icon, press Alt + T, or right-click and select "Eat Word" from the menu.
  • 3
    Save & Learn: Hit the Save button to store translations in your vocabulary. Sign in to sync across all your devices.
